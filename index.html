I am a Professor (博导) in University of Science and Technology of China. I received B.Eng. from Beihang University in July 2015 and PhD from National University of Singapore in August 2019. I have about 200 publications appeared in several top conferences such as SIGIR, SIGKDD, WWW, ACL, NeurIPS, ICML, and ICLR, as well as journals including Cell Reports, Cell Systems, TKDE, TOIS, and TPAMI. I received the Best Paper Honourable Mention in SIGIR 2021 and Best Poster Award in WWW 2018. My research interests include information retrieval, data mining, and multi-media analytics, particularly in machine learning techniques and applications such as large language model, causal inference, graph neural networks, recommender systems, AI4LifeScience, and FinTech. Moreover, I have served as the SAC/AC or SPC/PC-member for top-tier conferences including SIGIR, WWW, SIGKDD, NeurIPS, ICLR, ICML, ACL, and the invited reviewer for prestigious journals such as TOIS, TKDE, TPAMI, TNNLS, JASA, and Nature Sustainability.
